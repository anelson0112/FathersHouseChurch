Our Vision

The Father’s House is a non-denominational church that focuses on advancing God’s kingdom and preaching God’s word. We strive to create an environment of truth and compassion while promoting a biblical worldview. We encourage our members to love God with their whole heart while reaching out and caring for their fellow man.

God is affording The Father’s House Church the opportunity to own our property to fulfill the vision and call that the Lord has placed upon the Bishop and our ministry.

This vision is greater than all of us. We have made an impact that is beyond the visible. Ministries have been birthed and prospered around the world from our home. We intend to continue impacting the Peachtree Corners region and surrounding areas through outreach in the following capacities:

  • ONE RACE Atlanta (Bishop Garland Hunt, Co-Founder)
  • Religious Liberty Coalition (Bishop Garland Hunt, Co-Founder)
  • Prison Ministry - Ministered to over 1 million prisoners in 17 years.
  • Angel Tree Ministry - Supporting children of incarcerated parents.
  • Food Pantry ministry
  • Afterschool programs
  • The arts, dance and music
  • Recreation center for sports activities
  • In The Future: Build an additional structure for community recreation and outreach program use.
